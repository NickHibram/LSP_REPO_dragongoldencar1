I am an AI researcher, so my opinions on AI haven’t changed. It’s scary but also fascinating. It’s scary in the sense that it has so much potential for evil/harm. However, on the other side of that coin, it is fascinating how applicable it is to everything. It can handle the mundane work, allowing us to have far more time to be creative, which I believe is a key aspect of humanity: the ability to be curious. Regarding programming, I still think it’s fundamental to understand what you are coding; however, not using AI would be a for programming would be a mistake, as being afraid of its capabilities isn’t going to stop innovation, and not using it is only going to cause you to be behind. In regard to other coursework, it’s extremely helpful to learn. I often use it to make quizzes for myself or to check my writing.
